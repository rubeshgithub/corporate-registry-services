Manitoba corporations incorporated under *The Business Corporations Act (Manitoba)* must file an annual return with the Companies Office Manitoba every year. Like Quebec, Manitoba uses a **fixed calendar deadline** — all Manitoba corporations file by the same date.

## When Is the Manitoba Annual Return Due?

Manitoba annual returns are due **by March 31 each year** for corporations with a December 31 fiscal year-end. For other fiscal year-ends, the deadline is within 3 months of the fiscal year-end.

## Who Must File?

All corporations incorporated under *The Business Corporations Act (Manitoba)*, plus extra-provincial corporations registered in Manitoba, must file annually — including dormant and non-operating corporations.

## What Information Is Required?

- Full legal name and Manitoba Corporation Number
- Registered office address in Manitoba
- Names and addresses of current directors and officers
- Description of business activities

## How to File

Manitoba annual returns are filed through the **Companies Office Online** at [companiesoffice.gov.mb.ca](https://www.companiesoffice.gov.mb.ca).

**Steps:**
1. Access the Companies Office Online portal
2. Search for your corporation
3. Select "File Annual Return"
4. Confirm or update corporation information
5. Pay the $45 government fee
6. Receive confirmation

## Government Fee

The Manitoba government fee is **$45**.

## Consequences of Non-Compliance

Failure to file results in a non-compliance notice, followed by administrative dissolution if unresolved. A dissolved Manitoba corporation requires a revival application, additional fees, and processing time.
