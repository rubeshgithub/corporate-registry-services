Corporations incorporated in the Northwest Territories under the *Business Corporations Act (Northwest Territories)* must file an annual return with the NT Corporate Registry within 2 months of the corporation's anniversary date.

## When Is the NWT Annual Return Due?

NWT annual returns are due **within 2 months of the corporation's anniversary date**.

## Who Must File?

All corporations incorporated under the *Business Corporations Act (Northwest Territories)*, plus extra-provincial corporations registered in the NWT.

## What Information Is Required?

- Full legal name and NWT Corporation Number
- Registered office address in the Northwest Territories
- Names and addresses of all current directors and officers

## How to File

NWT annual returns are filed through the **NT Corporate Registry** at [justice.gov.nt.ca/en/corporate-registry](https://www.justice.gov.nt.ca/en/corporate-registry/).

**Steps:**
1. Access the NT Corporate Registry
2. Locate your corporation
3. File the annual return and update information
4. Pay the $50 government fee
5. Receive confirmation

## Government Fee

The NWT government fee is **$50**.
