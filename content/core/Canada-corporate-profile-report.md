# CRS Canada - Corporate Filings and Corporate Search [Source](https://CRS/Canada-corporate-profile-report.html) 

What is a Corporate Profile Report? It’s the official record of a corporation as maintained by federal or provincial registries in Canada. Reports vary slightly by jurisdiction but typically include: Current and former legal names of the corporation Incorporation or registration number Current status (active, dissolved, amalgamated, etc.) 
Date of incorporation or registration 
Registered office and mailing address 
Names and addresses of directors and officers (where available) Last document filed and key filing history Details of amalgamations or extra-provincial registrations

Why it matters. Unlike Articles of Incorporation — which show only how a company was originally created — a Corporate Profile Report displays the current official record. It’s essential for due diligence, compliance verification, and confirming a company’s existence before engaging in transactions or partnerships.
