Alberta Certificate of Good Standing. Available for $99 + applicable tax, all-in (including government charges).

A Alberta Certificate of Good Standing — also known as a Certificate of Status — is an official document issued by the Alberta Corporate Registry confirming that a corporation is legally registered, active, and compliant with all filing requirements under the Alberta Business Corporations Act.

This certificate is formal proof that a company is in good legal standing and remains authorized to operate. It is commonly required for banking, financing, extra-provincial registration, mergers, acquisitions, and official government or investor due diligence.

The certificate confirms:

- The corporation legally exists and is duly registered
- The corporation is active and has not been dissolved, discontinued, or amalgamated
- All required filings and annual returns are current and up to date
- The company is in good standing with the Alberta Corporate Registry

The Alberta Certificate of Good Standing is retrieved directly from the Alberta Corporate Registry, ensuring authenticity and accuracy.
