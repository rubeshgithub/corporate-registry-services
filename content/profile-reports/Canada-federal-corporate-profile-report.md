A Canada Federal Corporate Profile Report provides all current public record information available for a corporation registered under the Canada Business Corporations Act (CBCA). This official document is issued directly from the Corporations Canada registry and serves as the most up-to-date record of a company’s legal and operational status.

The report includes comprehensive details such as: Current and former legal names of the corporation 
Legal status (active, dissolved, or amalgamated) 
Registered office and mailing address 
Director and officer names and service addresses 
Date of incorporation and last document filed Corporations involved in amalgamation, if any 
Extra-provincial registration and filing details Name history and other historical information (if applicable) Unlike the Articles of Incorporation, which record the company’s initial formation, the Corporate Profile Report is considered the registry’s current and official information about the corporation.
