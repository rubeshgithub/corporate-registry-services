A Manitoba Corporate Profile Report provides complete, current public record information on any corporation registered under the Manitoba Business Corporations Act and recorded in the Manitoba Companies Office.

## What's Included

- Current and historical legal names of the corporation
- Corporate registry number and registration details
- Registered office and mailing address
- Names and addresses of directors and officers
- Legal status (active, dissolved, or struck)
- Date of incorporation or registration
- Most recent annual return filing
- Extra-provincial registration information (if applicable)

The Manitoba Corporate Profile Report is retrieved directly from the Manitoba Companies Office to ensure accuracy and authenticity.
