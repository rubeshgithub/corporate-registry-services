A New Brunswick Corporate Profile Report provides complete, current public record information on any corporation registered under the Business Corporations Act and recorded in the New Brunswick Corporate Registry.

The New Brunswick report is a key document that verifies a company's existence, status, and management structure. It is often required for due diligence, financial transactions, legal reviews, or cross-provincial registrations.

## What's Included

- Current and historical legal names of the corporation
- Corporate registry number and registration details
- Registered office and mailing address
- Names and addresses of directors and officers
- Legal status (active, dissolved, or struck)
- Date of incorporation or registration in New Brunswick
- Most recent annual return filing
- Extra-provincial registration information (if applicable)

The New Brunswick Corporate Profile Report is retrieved directly from the New Brunswick Corporate Registry to ensure accuracy and authenticity. It provides verified data to confirm the identity and good standing of a business operating in New Brunswick.
