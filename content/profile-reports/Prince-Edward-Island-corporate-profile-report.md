A Prince Edward Island Corporate Profile Report provides complete, current public record information on any corporation registered under the Prince Edward Island Business Corporations Act and recorded in the PEI Corporate Registry.

## What's Included

- Current and historical legal names of the corporation
- Corporate registry number and registration details
- Registered office and mailing address
- Names and addresses of directors and officers
- Legal status (active, dissolved, or struck)
- Date of incorporation or registration
- Most recent annual return filing
- Extra-provincial registration information (if applicable)

The Prince Edward Island Corporate Profile Report is retrieved directly from the PEI Corporate Registry to ensure accuracy and authenticity.
