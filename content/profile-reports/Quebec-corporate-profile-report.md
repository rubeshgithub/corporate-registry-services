A Quebec Corporate Profile Report provides complete, current public record information on any corporation registered with the Quebec Enterprise Register (REQ) - Registre des entreprises du Québec.

The Quebec report is a key document that verifies a company's existence, status, and management structure. It is often required for due diligence, financial transactions, legal reviews, or cross-provincial registrations.

## What's Included

- Current and historical legal names of the corporation (French and English)
- NEQ (Numéro d'entreprise du Québec) and registration details
- Registered office and mailing address
- Names and addresses of directors and officers
- Legal status (active, dissolved, or struck)
- Date of incorporation or registration in Quebec
- Most recent annual return filing (déclaration annuelle)
- Extra-provincial registration information (if applicable)

The Quebec Corporate Profile Report is retrieved directly from the Quebec Enterprise Register (REQ) to ensure accuracy and authenticity.
